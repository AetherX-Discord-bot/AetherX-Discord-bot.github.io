Music Commands

!play [song/URL] - Plays music from YouTube or Spotify, or searches for the song.
!skip - Skips the currently playing song.
!stop - Stops the music and clears the queue.
!pause - Pauses the currently playing song.
!resume - Resumes a paused song.
!queue - Displays the current song queue.
!skipto [number] - Skips to the song at the specified position in the queue.
!volume [1-100] - Sets the volume of the music player.
!nowplaying - Displays the currently playing song.
!disconnect - Makes the bot leave the voice channel.
!shuffle - Shuffles the current song queue.
!lyrics [song name] - Fetches and displays the lyrics for the specified song.
!seek [time] - Jumps to a specific time in the currently playing song.
!repeat - Toggles repeat for the current song or the entire queue.
!playlist [playlist name/URL] - Plays a specified playlist from a supported service.
